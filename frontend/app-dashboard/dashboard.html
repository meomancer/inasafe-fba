Welcome to the InaSAFE Forecast Based Action dashboard. This site is our first prototype of a hazard impact based forecast dashboard to trigger Early Action.

To get started, use the green hazard selector icon below to select a historical or forecast hazard event (valid dates have circles around them).

This site is developed in collaboration with the Red Cross Red Crescent Climate Centre with funding from the World Bank GFDRR Challenge Fund and contributing to the Indonesia Red Cross Early Action protocol for hazards.

Click the download button on the top right of the hazard forecast summary to download the hazard impact forecast report.
Click the Add Hazard button on the right side of the navigation bar to add your hazard forecast layer.
Click the Browse Hazard button on the bottom right of InaSAFE FBA to browse hazard forecasts.
